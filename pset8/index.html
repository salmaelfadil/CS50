Hello, Lang Learner

What is this website?

Have you ever wanted to learn a language but felt lost, did not know where to start and felt overwhelmed by the amount of sources available online?
This website is your ultimate guide to language learning.
It lists all of the available language learning platforms as well as their reviews and finds the one tailored for your goals.
Our platform also provides users with the ability to interact with one another to get hands on experience from language learners all around the globe
And much much more! Join our community now!

Who am I?

I am a cs50 student who speaks four languages;

  • Arabic
  • English
  • French
  • Spanish

To study each thoroughly, I had to go through numerous learning platforms that were useless, time and money consuming.
As such, I wanted to facilitate the experience for fellow language learners around the world
Also, I aim to create a community that can share ideas and provide others with help I long saught for.
